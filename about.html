About the App

The ripay app promotes Financial inclusion by extending Credit to Micro , Small & Medium Businesses and entrepreneurs without access to collateral directly to their mobile phones anytime, anywhere through their Mobile Money Wallets.

This is done through:

Business Loans

These loans are specifically intended for small and medium entrepreneurs. The loans range from between UGX 100,000 to UGX 1,000,000, with a payment duration of
between 8 to 28 days and interest rates of
between 5% to 20%.

Smartphone Asset Financing

This loans are extended to entrepreneurs in the logistics sector who work for ride hailing companies. The clients make a payment towards the asset finance loan daily of between UGX 3,200 and UGX 5,000 for up to 6 months depending on the type of smartphone and payment duration.

With Smartphone asset financing:
The smartphones are insured against theft and damage and are installed with the Ripay proprietary loan and collection apps. In the event delayed payment, the collection app blocks the borrower from using the phone with the exception of payment for the due loan instalment payments.
