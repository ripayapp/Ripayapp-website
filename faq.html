1.How do I apply for a loan?

How to apply for a Ripay loan through the Ripay mobile application
These are the steps to follow if you wish to apply for a Ripay Loan through the Ripay app:
Installing the Ripay app on your android phone:
a) Go to Google play store and search for Ripay app
b) Click on the install button
c) Once installed, click on Open
d) Allow the Ripay app to make /manage calls / read messages
e) Enter your MTN/Airtel line number to register
Once done, you will set a pin for logging the app.
Log in to the app using the necessary credentials. You shall see that your loan/credit limit is displayed on the app.
a)Select ‘My Loans’.
b)Select ‘Request Loan’.
c)Enter the amount you wish to borrow.
d)Select ‘Continue’

4.Whom do we share your info with and why?

We may disclose some or all of the data that we collect from you when you download or use the App to credit reference bureaus.
The purpose of this is to verify your credit history if you apply for a loan so that we can consider it in our loan decision,
and if you receive a loan, to deter and discourage you from defaulting on the repayment of the loan.

Your information may be shared with and processed by staff operating inside or outside of Uganda who work for us or for one of our suppliers.
These staff need access to all of the information that we collect about you as they may be engaged in providing you with personalized support
and processing your loan applications. These staff members are contractually obligated to treat your data confidentially and securely
in accordance with this Data Privacy Policy. By using and submitting your data on the App, you agree to this sharing and processing of your data.
We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

Besides sharing your data with credit reference bureaus, staff and suppliers, we do not share information that personally identifies you unless
you give us your permission in writing or unless we are under a duty to do so in order to comply with any legal or regulatory obligation or request.
Personal information is information like your name, phone number, email address that can by it be used to contact you or identifies who you are.

We may work with third parties that pay us to issue advertisements on Ripay, providing us with revenues that make it possible to
offer our services free of charge to our users. We and our advertising partners want the advertisements on Ripay to be relevant,
interesting and ideally helpful for you or your business. With this in mind, we use the information that we have
about you and your business to show you advertisements that meet these goals. We do not share your Personal
information unless you give us permission. See definition above for Personal Information.

7.What is Investo?

“Investo” is an Ripay product that enables customers to invest safely and securely by seeing precisely where your money
goes and how much to expect, all in real-time.

8.What makes Investo different?

We are dedicated to providing our investors with ultimate transparency.
The mechanics of the platform allows you to see the effect you are having in real-time,

9.Who can Invest?

Anyone who has a steady source of income, a valid government-issued ID (International Passport, National ID)

10.How do I start investing?

To begin investing, you must first sign up on for Investo. Once you have provided the required details,
your account will go through the verification process. Upon verification, you will be required to sign
in to the portal with your email address and password. Once you have done this, all you need to do is
create investments by making payment either through card payment or bank transfer.

11.What is the minimum and maximum amount I can invest?

The minimum investment amount is UGX 50,000.00 while the maximum investment amount allowed on the notes is UGX 100,000,000.
If you wish to create an investment above UGX 100,000,000, we require that an email be sent to katongh@ryrainvest.com.
