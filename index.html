How it works

Simply follow the steps below to get started.

1. Download the app

Download the RIPAY app from Google Play Store on your Android device.

2. Create your account

Simply fill out your details in less than a minute and submit for a quick review.

3. Update your profile

Modular and interchangable componente between layouts and even demos.

4. Check Eligibility

Check Eligibility to see the amount of loan you are eligible to borrow in seconds.

5. Apply for loan

Loans will be sent to your provided account in 5 minutes or less.

6. Receive funds in your bank account

Loans can be repaid via the App or directly to your personalized bank account. Build your credit score to qualify for higher loans.

Products

By using our App we can offer you the following.

Credit

The Ripay platform gives Businesses and entrepreneur’s access to credit and the loan amount, cycle and repayment are configurable in the system to fit the needs of the borrower.
